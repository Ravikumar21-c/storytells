Book Title:-

ARISE, AWAKE

Author's Note:

I first wrote for a newspaper when i was 18 year old. It started out as a lark, a desire to see my name in print. But whrn i send my carefully typed article to the editor of 'The Times Of India' , and all i received for my effort was a 'rejection slip'. I tried agin and agin , and agin. Each time I got the same impersonal response. I should have got the message. That i am not 'good enough'. Instead, mujhe aur josh aa gaya. It become a sort of a game for me.
